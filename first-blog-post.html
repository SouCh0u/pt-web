My Top 5 Favourite Goddesses

1. Diana

Diana (also known as Atermis in Greek mythology) is the Roman Goddess of the moon.

2. Kuan Yin

Kuan Yin (also spelt Quan Yin) is the Chinese Goddess of compassion.

3. Hecate

Hecate is the Greek goddess of the moon.

4. Selene

Selene (also known as Luna in Roman mythology) is the Greek goddess of the moon.

5. Brigid

Brigid is the Celtic Goddess of creativity.
